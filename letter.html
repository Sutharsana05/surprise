A letter for you
SANA
Sometimes you are aggressive, strong, full of argue
Sometimes you are beautiful, kind, a gentle cry baby.
· · ♥ · ·
The point is
none of us are easy to be with.
It is who is willing to stay,
commit to understand you,
and actually wants to grow with you.
You are not difficult to love.
You just haven't met someone
mature enough to handle your depth.
· · ♥ · ·
Your one single action will make me
come back again
but you are not nerdy enough
to understand that.
